Why Yubi AppGate

Hardware Security for Your Apps

Replace PINs and patterns with real cryptographic authentication backed by a physical key you control.

🔑

YubiKey Authentication

Uses HMAC-SHA1 challenge-response with your YubiKey. Authentication happens on the hardware key itself — secrets never leave the device.

📡

NFC & USB‑C

Tap your YubiKey over NFC or connect via USB-C. Both transport methods are fully supported with automatic detection.

🛡️

System-wide Protection

Once enabled, protected apps require YubiKey authentication no matter how they're launched — from the home screen, recent apps, or other apps.

🚫

No Cloud Required

Everything stays on your device. No accounts to create, no data uploaded, no internet connection needed for authentication.

⚡

Instant Unlock

The challenge-response handshake completes in under a second. Tap and go — no typing, no waiting.

🧩

Uninstall Protection

Optional device administrator mode prevents the app from being silently uninstalled by someone with physical access to your phone.
